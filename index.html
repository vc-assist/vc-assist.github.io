VC Assist is the app that enables students to build effective study habits by providing information that a student should have easy access to.

How to use VC Assist

Sign in

 


Sign in using your warriorlife username and password! If you are logged out, sign in quickly using biometric authentication!

View

 


View your color day, GPA, and current date. Join your current zoom room class in a single tap. Check off completed homework with an in-app checklist.

Use

 


Use your data with a checklist, Learn@VCS link to class, and add your homework to Apple Calendar! We include hyperlinks and highlight keywords.



New Feature

Grade Calculator

  This feature allows users accurately approximate what grade is neccessary on a test, quiz, or assignment for them to reach a certain grade. You can also see the breakdown of the weights in each class to better understand where to allocate your resources to raise your grade.
